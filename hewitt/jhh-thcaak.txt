"The Hunting Chorus Ahmed al Kamal
and Holy Sherman York" (not dated)
Written and Composed by John Hill Hewitt, 1801-1890

John Hill Hewitt papers #31
Special Collections,

||: When morning mists obscure the day
O’er moon and mountains gray
Up cries the foresters away
Thus merrily we go. :||

Our hound the stag assail
