"Pickaninnies' Jubilee" (1897)
Schottische for Pianoforte
[Words and Music]
by
Fred[erick]. H. Day

Boston/New York/Chicago: White-Smith Music Publishing Co.
Plate No. 10438-4
[Source: MUM00682]

Go and meet yer Honey at the Picaninnies’ Jubilee tonight.
All de yaller gals of old Black town are gwine to be there
and wont there be a terrible sight?
Dars gwine to be a cake walk, now we give you one and all and invite
To dance and sing by the light of the moon
at the Pickaninnies Jubilee tonight.
