"Should Auld Acquaintence Be Forgot" (circa May 1799)
[aka "Auld Lang Syne"]
Words and Music -- anonymous Scotch air
[Source: facsimile on page 339, song info on pages 115-117,
from "The Book of World-Famous Music" by James J. Fuld]

||: Should auld acquaintence be forgot, 
And never brought to mind? 
Should auld acquaintence be forgot 
And days of auld lang syne? :||
||:For auld  lang syne my dear 
For auld lang syne, 
We’ll take a cup o’ kindness yet 
For auld lang syne. :||
