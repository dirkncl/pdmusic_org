1.
When o'er the distant Eastern Hills,
The rays of morn appear
And swift the rising glory fills
Earth's vast yet narrow'd sphere;
We thank Thee, for the light and day,
And for Earth's varied store;
We thank Thee, Lord, in ev'ry way,
And still Thy name adore!
