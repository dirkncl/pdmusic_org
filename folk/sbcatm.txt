"She'll Be Comin' Around the Mountain"
Tradition American Folksong

1.
She'll be comin' around the mountain when she comes,
She'll be comin' around the mountain when she comes,
She'll be comin' around the mountain,
She'll be comin' around the mountain,
She'll be comin' around the mountain when she comes.

2.
She'll be drivin' six white horses when she comes, etc.

3.
We will all go out to meet her when she comes, etc.

4.
We will have chicken an' dumplin's when she comes, etc.

5.
She'll be reelin' an a-rockin' when she comes, etc.

6.
We'll shout glory hallelujah when she comes, etc.
