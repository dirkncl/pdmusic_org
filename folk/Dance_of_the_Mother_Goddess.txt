"Dance of the Mother Goddess" (Folk Song)
Words and Melody from Gujarat west India
[Source: page 15 from "Folk Songs from India"]

[Original Indian words]
Sachhi re maari satre bhavani maa,
ambe bhavanimaa uttri ramva saathe rang relia.

[English translation]
Holy mother we dance for you, you are true,
dance for you, you are true, come and join our dancing
beautif’lly dressed.
