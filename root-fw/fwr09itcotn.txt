"In the Calm of the Night" (1909)
[A Short Song-Study]
by Frederic Woodman Root, 1846-1916

[Source: p. 48 of "Guide for the Male Voice"]

[BARITONE or BASS]
In the calm of the night,
'Neath the moon's silv'ry light,
Hear the nightengale's note
Thro' the forest float.

[ALTO or TENOR]
In the calm of the night,
'Neath the moon's silv'ry light,
Hear the nightengale's note
Thro' the forest float.
