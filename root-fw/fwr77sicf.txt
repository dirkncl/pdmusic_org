"Solfeggio in Canon Form" (1877)
by Frederic Woodman Root, 1846-1916

[Source: page 109-110 from "The New Song Era"]

Divide the voices into two Divisions. Let the second Division
begin _one measure after the first_, both going together
through the piece on measure apart:  let the accompaniment
be played with the first Division. Use the "ah" after learning
the piece thoroughly with syllables.
