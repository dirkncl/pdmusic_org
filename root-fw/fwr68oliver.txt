"Oliver" S. M. (9 June 1868)
by Frederic Woodman Root, 1846-1916

[Source: page 222, in the "New Tunes" section,
of "The Triumph" by George F. Root]

1.
Come, sound His praise a-broad,
And hymns of glo-ry sing;
Je-ho-vah is a sov'-reign God,
The u-ni-ver-sal King.

2.
He formed the deeps un-known,
He gave the seas their bound;
The wat-ery worlds are all His own,
And all the sol-id ground.
