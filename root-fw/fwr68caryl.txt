"Caryl" (9 June 1868)
6s & 4 (Hymn)
Words by James R. Murray
[Music/melody?] From Robert Franz [Knauth], 1815-1892
[Arranged?] by Frederick Woodman Root, 1846-1916

[Source: page 254 of "The Triumph" by Geo. F. Root]

1.
O, Father unto Thee,
Who loveth such as me,
I offer for grateful praise
For all my days.

2.
For days when I am glad,
For days when I am sad,
For days of good or ill
I praise Thee still.
