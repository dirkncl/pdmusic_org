On, Wisconsin!
On, Wisconsin!
Plunge— right— thru— that— line!
Run the ball clear ’round Chicago.*—
[* or "Minnesota"]
A touchdown sure this time.
On, Wisconsin!
On, Wisconsin!
Fight on for her fame.
Fight! fellows! Fight!
And we will win this game.
