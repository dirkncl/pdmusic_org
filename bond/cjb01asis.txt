To Mrs. O. L. Fox
"A Study in Symbols" (1901)
Words by Clarence Urmy
Music by Carrie Jacobs-Bond, 1862-1946

[Source: pp. 30-31 from "Songs Everybody Sings"]

1.
From blue to red,
From red to gold,
From gold to gray--
So turns the sky;
So fades the light,
So ends the day.

2.
From ease to strife,
From strife to pain,
From pain to peace--
So life shall wane;
So grief decline,
So toll shall cease.
