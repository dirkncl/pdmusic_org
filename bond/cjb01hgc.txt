To Miss Julia Putnam
"Her Greatest Charm" (1901)
Words -- Anonymous
Music by Carrie Jacobs-Bond, 1862-1946

[Source: p. 79 from "Songs Everybody Sings"]

The violet lingers in her eye, 
The roses on her cheek, 
Her dainty lips of poppy leaf with pearls play hide and seek; 
But the dearest of the blossoms, 
Which her many charms disclose, 
Is the funny little dandelion freckle on her nose.
