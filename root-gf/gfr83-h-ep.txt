"Evening Prayer" (1883)
[Hymn]
Words by James Ramsey Murray, 1841-1905
Music by George Frederick Root, 1820-1895
[page 83 of ? from ?]

John Church & Co.
[Source: 06230@LoC]

1.
Into Thy loving care,
Into Thy keeping,
Thou who are everywhere,
Take us while sleeping.

2.
Let Thine almighty arm
Closely enfold us;
Nothing can hurt or harm
Whilts Thou dost hold us.

3.
Thro' all the deathlike night
Thy watch be given,
Bring me to morning light
Here or in heaven.
